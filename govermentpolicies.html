Goverment Policies

Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA)

This act provides guaranteed wage employment for unskilled manual work to rural households for a minimum of 100 days in a financial year.

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Gramin (PMAY-G)

This scheme aims to provide pucca houses with basic amenities to all houseless households and those living in kutcha or dilapidated houses in rural areas.

Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY)

This scheme focuses on skill development in rural areas, providing vocational training and placement assistance to rural youth.

Indira Gandhi National Old Age Pension Scheme (NSAP)

This scheme provides financial asistance to the elderly in rural areas.

Saansad Adarsh Gram Yojana (SAGY)

This scheme encourages Members of Parliament to adopt villages and work towards their holistic development.
